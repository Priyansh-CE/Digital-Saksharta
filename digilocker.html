DigiLocker

Digital Document Storage

What is DigiLocker?

DigiLocker is a digital document wallet provided by the Government of India. It allows citizens to store, access and share important documents like Aadhaar, marksheets, certificates and licenses anytime from anywhere.

Benefits of DigiLocker

Digital Storage

Store important documents securely without carrying physical copies.

Anytime Access

Access your documents anytime using smartphone or computer.

Verified Documents

Government issued documents are digitally verified.

DigiLocker Registration Process

01

Visit the official DigiLocker website or install the mobile application.

02

Create an account using your mobile number and Aadhaar.

03

Verify OTP and complete registration.

04

Access your digital documents from your account.

How to Upload Documents

Login

Sign in to your DigiLocker account using your registered mobile number.

Upload

Click on "Uploaded Documents" and upload PDF, JPG or PNG files.

Organize

Arrange documents into folders for easy access whenever needed.

How Students Can Use DigiLocker

Mark Sheets

Store board exam mark sheets and certificates digitally.

College Admission

Submit verified documents during admission without photocopies.

Scholarships

Upload digital documents while applying for scholarships.

Identity Proof

Keep Aadhaar, PAN Card, Driving Licence and other IDs safely.

Security Tips

Strong Password

Use a unique password and never share it with anyone.

Keep OTP Secret

Never share your OTP or login verification code.

Official Website

Always access DigiLocker through official websites or apps.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is DigiLocker free?

Yes. DigiLocker is a free service provided by the Government of India.


2. Can I upload my own documents?

Yes. You can upload personal documents and keep them securely in your account.


3. Are DigiLocker documents valid?

Government-issued digital documents available in DigiLocker are legally valid for many official purposes.
