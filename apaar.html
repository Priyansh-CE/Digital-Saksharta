APAAR ID

Student Academic Identity

What is APAAR ID?

APAAR ID is a unique student identification system introduced under the National Education Policy (NEP 2020). It helps students maintain lifelong academic records digitally and securely.

Benefits of APAAR ID

🎓 One Student - One Academic Identity
📚 Digital Academic Records
🏫 Easy School & College Transfers
📄 Secure Certificate Management
🌐 National Recognition
🔒 Safe & Paperless Education Records

How to Get APAAR ID

1

Visit your school or college.

2

Provide Aadhaar details with consent.

3

Your institution creates your APAAR ID.

4

Your academic records are linked securely.

Important Points

  • ✅ One APAAR ID for every student.
  • ✅ Helps maintain lifelong academic records.
  • ✅ Supports Digital India & NEP 2020.
  • ✅ Makes education services paperless.
  • ✅ Useful for admissions and record verification.
