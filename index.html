Digital Safety Tips

Create Strong Passwords

Use a combination of uppercase, lowercase, numbers and special characters.

Beware of Phishing

Never click suspicious emails or unknown links.

Keep Apps Updated

Regular updates improve security and performance.

Protect Personal Data

Never share OTP, passwords or banking details online.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Digital Literacy?

Digital literacy is the ability to use computers, smartphones, the internet and digital services safely and effectively.

Who can use this portal?

This platform is designed for school students, teachers and anyone interested in learning digital skills.

Is it free?
