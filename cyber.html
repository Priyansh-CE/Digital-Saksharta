Cyber Safety Tips

Strong Password

Use at least 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase, numbers and symbols.

Never Share OTP

Banks never ask for OTP, PIN or Password.

Avoid Phishing

Never click suspicious emails or unknown links.

Enable 2FA

Turn on Two-Factor Authentication for extra account security.

Beware of Online Frauds

Never share OTP, ATM PIN, Aadhaar details, passwords or bank information. Verify every website before making online payments.
