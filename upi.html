UPI

Fast & Secure Payments

What is UPI?

UPI (Unified Payments Interface) is a digital payment system that allows instant money transfer between bank accounts using mobile applications.

Popular UPI Apps

Google Pay

PhonePe

Paytm

BHIM UPI

How to Make a Payment

1

Open your UPI App.

2

Select Send Money or Scan QR Code.

3

Enter the amount.

4

Verify details and enter your UPI PIN.

UPI Safety Tips

🔒 Never share your UPI PIN.
📲 Use only trusted UPI apps.
❌ Never enter your PIN to receive money.
📵 Ignore unknown payment requests.
🛡️ Enable screen lock on your phone.
⚠️ Verify the receiver before payment.

Common UPI Frauds

  • ❌ Fake QR Codes
  • ❌ Fake Customer Care Calls
  • ❌ Screen Sharing Apps
  • ❌ OTP Scams
  • ❌ Reward Point Scams
  • ❌ Fake Payment Links

Stay Alert, Stay Secure

Never share OTP or UPI PIN with anyone. Always verify before making payments.
