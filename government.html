What are Government Digital Services?

Government Digital Services allow citizens to access public services online without visiting government offices. They save time, reduce paperwork and improve transparency.

Aadhaar

Identity verification and updates.

DigiLocker

Store and access digital documents.

UMANG

Access multiple government services in one app.

Ayushman Bharat

Health scheme information and benefits.

Tips for Safe Usage

  • ✅ Use only official government websites and apps.
  • ✅ Never share OTP or passwords.
  • ✅ Verify website URLs before entering personal details.
  • ✅ Keep Aadhaar and mobile number updated.
  • ✅ Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
