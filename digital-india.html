What is Digital India?

Digital India is a flagship programme launched by the Government of India to improve online infrastructure, increase internet connectivity and make government services available electronically to every citizen.

Objectives

Digital Infrastructure

Provide high-speed internet access across India.

Digital Services

Deliver government services online efficiently.

Digital Literacy

Help every citizen learn digital skills.
