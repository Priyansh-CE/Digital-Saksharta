Academic Bank of Credits (ABC ID)

ABC ID helps students securely store, transfer and redeem academic credits across higher education institutions under the National Education Policy (NEP 2020).

Digital Academic Credits

What is ABC ID?

The Academic Bank of Credits (ABC) is a digital platform where students can securely store academic credits earned from recognized educational institutions. These credits can later be transferred or redeemed while pursuing higher education.

Benefits of ABC ID

📚 Store Academic Credits Securely
🏫 Easy College Transfer
🎓 Flexible Learning
🔄 Credit Transfer Between Institutions
📄 Digital Academic Records
🌐 Supports NEP 2020

How ABC ID Works

1

Create an ABC ID through the official student portal.

2

Link your educational institution.

3

Academic credits are stored digitally.

4

Redeem or transfer credits when required.

Advantages for Students

✔ Continue studies without losing credits.
✔ Better flexibility in higher education.
✔ Secure digital record keeping.
✔ National recognition of credits.
✔ Easier admissions and verification.
✔ Supports lifelong learning.

Important Information

  • ✅ Available for students enrolled in recognized institutions.
  • ✅ Works with the National Education Policy (NEP 2020).
  • ✅ Academic credits remain digitally accessible.
  • ✅ Helps students move between institutions more easily.
  • ✅ Reduces paperwork and improves transparency.
