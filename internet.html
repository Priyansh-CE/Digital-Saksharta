Safe Internet

Smart Browsing

Why Internet Safety Matters

Internet safety helps protect your personal information, prevents cybercrime, and ensures a secure online experience while studying, shopping, or using digital services.

Best Practices

Use Strong Passwords

Create unique passwords with letters, numbers and symbols.

Protect Privacy

Never share personal information with strangers online.

Enable Security

Use antivirus software and keep devices updated.

Safe Networks

Avoid logging into important accounts on public Wi-Fi.

Common Online Threats

🎣 Phishing Emails
🦠 Malware & Viruses
💳 Fake Shopping Websites
📱 Social Media Scams
🎁 Fake Prize Messages
👤 Identity Theft

Internet Safety Checklist

  • ✅ Check website URLs before entering personal details.
  • ✅ Never share passwords or OTPs.
  • ✅ Download files only from trusted websites.
  • ✅ Think before clicking unknown links.
  • ✅ Log out from shared computers.
  • ✅ Report suspicious activity immediately.

Think Before You Click

Being careful online helps protect your identity, money and personal information.
