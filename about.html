About Our Project

Empowering Students Through Digital Literacy

Digital Saksharta is an educational initiative designed to help students understand digital technologies, cyber security, government digital services and responsible internet usage.

Digital Literacy

What is Digital Literacy?

Digital Skills

Digital literacy means the ability to use computers, smartphones, the internet and digital services safely, effectively and responsibly.

Internet Awareness

Understand online communication, digital information, social media and internet safety.

Cyber Safety

Protect yourself from cyber attacks, online fraud, identity theft and phishing scams.

🇮🇳 Digital India Mission

Digital India is a flagship initiative of the Government of India that aims to transform the country into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy. It promotes digital infrastructure, digital services and digital literacy for every citizen.

Project Objectives

Digital Education

Provide students with practical knowledge of digital technologies and online services.

Cyber Awareness

Promote safe internet practices and protection against cyber crimes.

Government Services

Introduce students to Aadhaar, DigiLocker, UPI, APAAR ID, ABC ID and other digital services.

Benefits of Digital Literacy

Smart Technology Use

Learn to use smartphones, computers and digital tools confidently.

Online Safety

Stay protected from scams, fake websites and online fraud.

Career Readiness

Build digital skills required for education and future careers.

Digital Inclusion

Encourage everyone to participate confidently in the digital world.
