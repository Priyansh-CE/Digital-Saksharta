Scholarship

Education Support

Who Can Apply?

School Students

Primary, Secondary and Higher Secondary students.

College Students

Diploma, Engineering, Degree and PG students.

Eligible Categories

Merit, SC, ST, OBC, EWS and other eligible schemes.

Application Process

1

Register on the Scholarship Portal.

2

Fill personal and educational details.

3

Upload required documents.

4

Submit application and track status.

Required Documents

📄 Aadhaar Card
🏦 Bank Passbook
🎓 Marksheet
📷 Passport Photo
🏠 Income Certificate
📑 Caste Certificate (if applicable)

Common Mistakes

  • ❌ Wrong Aadhaar Number
  • ❌ Incorrect Bank Details
  • ❌ Blurred Documents
  • ❌ Missing Signature
  • ❌ Applying after deadline

Tips

✔ Apply before deadline.
✔ Verify all information.
✔ Keep scanned documents ready.
✔ Track application regularly.
